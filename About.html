Some examples of movies in this section include The Godfather, Schindler's List, and The Shawshank Redemption.
The second section displays movies with ratings of 8-5, which are still highly regarded but may not have achieved the same level of critical acclaim as those in the first section. These movies are still considered to be very good, and are often popular with audiences. Examples of movies in this section include The Dark Knight, Fight Club, and The Matrix.
